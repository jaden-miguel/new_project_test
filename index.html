About Crowns by Design

Crowns by Design is a locally owned and operated dental laboratory serving dentists throughout the Pacific Northwest. With over 20 years of experience, our master technicians combine artistry and advanced technology to create custom restorations that fit perfectly and look beautiful.

Our Services

Crown & Bridge

High-strength zirconia, e.max, and layered porcelain restorations with precise margins.

Implant Solutions

Custom abutments and screw-retained crowns designed for long-term success.

Digital Dentistry

We accept all major intra-oral scans and provide rapid turnaround times.

Shade Matching

On-site or virtual shade consultations to achieve lifelike esthetics.
