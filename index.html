Premium Dental Laboratory

Committed to Providing You with the Best Service and Care

Since 1985, Crowns by Design has been a family-run business providing dentists and patients with exceptional dental restorations.

We are conversant in many forms of dental restoration and can help you safely, profitably navigate the complex, ever-changing landscape of technology available today.

Crowns by Design is in the business of forging long-term relationships based on our commitment to quality, professionalism, and trust.

Our Promise to You

We are committed to treating you as we would want to be treated. We stand behind every project we create with pride. Every piece we make comes with a signed card by the person who made it, representing our belief that the product is of the highest quality and will surpass your expectations today and for years to come.
