About Crowns by Design

Excellence in dental laboratory services since establishment

Your Trusted Dental Laboratory Partner

Located in the heart of Bellingham, Washington, Crowns by Design has been serving dental professionals and their patients with exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail. Our state-of-the-art facility combines traditional techniques with cutting-edge technology to deliver superior dental restorations.

We understand that every smile is unique, which is why we work closely with dental practices to ensure each restoration meets the highest standards of quality, fit, and aesthetics.
